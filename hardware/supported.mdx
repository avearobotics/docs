This is the hardware we support with prebuilt adapters today. Running something else? You can integrate it over standard ROS 2 — see [Integrate your own adapter](/integration/overview) — or [ask us](https://avea-robotics.slack.com) and we'll add it.

## Compute

- x86 machines
- Jetson AGX Orin Developer Kit

## Cameras

- **Stereolabs:** ZED and ZED X Series
- **RealSense:** D435i, D405
- Any UVC camera
