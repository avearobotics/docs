These are known-good starting configs for common bimanual setups. Download the one that matches your hardware, then fill in the placeholders before launch.

<Note>
  Each file has placeholders to replace: `system.license_key` (`<LICENSE_KEY>`),
  the camera `serial_number` fields, and the per-arm CAN `connection.port`
  (YAM) where applicable. Generate a license key in the
  [dashboard](https://dashboard.avearobotics.com/dashboard/robots).
</Note>

## Bimanual YAM

2× RealSense D405 wrist cameras + 1× D405 overhead (swappable for D455/D435i). Works out of the box with the `MolmoAct2-Bimanual-Yam` checkpoint.
