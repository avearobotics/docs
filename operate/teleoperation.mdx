An operator uses the Sentinel app on a Meta Quest 3 or 3S to drive a connected robot.

## Session flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a robot">
    Sign in to the operator app and choose an available robot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the camera views">
    Confirm that each required camera is live and shows the expected view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arm the robot">
    Put the robot into its ready state. Keep the workspace clear while the robot arms and moves to its start pose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start teleoperation">
    Hold the configured deadman control to drive. Releasing it stops teleoperation and returns the robot to its ready state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="End the session">
    Stop teleoperation, disarm the robot, and close the session when collection is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Keep a physical emergency stop within reach whenever the robot can move. Do not rely on the headset or network as the only emergency-stop path.
</Warning>
